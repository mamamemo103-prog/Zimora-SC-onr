Privacy Policy

Effective date · March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Zimora (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use our marketing website and linked free-to-play game experiences. Zimora is an entertainment product that uses virtual coins only—there is no real-money gaming on this site.

Information we may collect

  • Technical data: such as browser type, device category, and coarse region inferred from network information, to keep the service stable and secure.
  • Communications: if you email us, we process the content of your message and contact details solely to respond.
  • Local storage: some linked games may store progress or virtual coin totals in your browser (for example, via sessionStorage or similar APIs) so sessions feel continuous.

How we use information

We use information to operate and improve Zimora, understand aggregate usage, troubleshoot errors, comply with law, and respond to requests. We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies and similar technologies

We may use essential technologies required for basic site function. If we introduce analytics or optional cookies in the future, we will describe them here and provide choices where required by law.

Third parties

Embedded games or fonts loaded from third-party hosts may process technical data according to their own policies. We recommend reviewing those services if you need detail beyond what Zimora controls on this domain.

Children

Zimora is not directed to children. Where local law requires a minimum age for social-style entertainment, you must meet that age to use the service responsibly.

Security

We apply reasonable safeguards for a lightweight marketing site. No method of transmission over the Internet is perfectly secure; please use unique passwords where applicable and contact us if you notice abuse.

Your choices

You may contact us to ask questions about this Policy or to exercise rights available in your region, subject to verification. See the Contact page.

Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page.
