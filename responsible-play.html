Responsible play

March 2026 · Entertainment first · Virtual coins only

Zimora is built for free-to-play fun. Even without real-money wagering, social-style games deserve mindful habits. This page outlines healthy play principles and how Zimora keeps expectations clear.

Remember: virtual coins

Every experience linked from Zimora uses virtual coins or similar markers for pacing and delight. Those coins cannot be cashed out, traded for value, or used as money. If anything suggests otherwise, discontinue use and contact us.

Healthy session tips

  • Set a time limit before you open a lobby—and stand up when the timer ends.
  • Play in a comfortable environment; bright motion and sound can be intense on small screens.
  • If play stops feeling light, switch activities. Entertainment should recharge you, not drain you.
  • Keep young viewers supervised according to your household rules and local guidance.

No purchase pressure on this site

Browsing Zimora and opening catalog titles does not require payment. Individual games may present optional promotional patterns; treat them as separate experiences and use only what you are comfortable with.

When to pause

Step back if you notice irritability, lost time, or compulsion to “chase” outcomes—even with virtual coins. A break is a feature, not a flaw. Pair gaming with sleep, movement, and offline connection.

Get support

If you or someone you care about needs help with gaming habits, seek qualified professionals in your region. You can always reach Zimora through the Contact page for product-related questions.
